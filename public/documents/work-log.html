Project Summary

The Monroe Resource Hub is a comprehensive community platform designed to connect residents of Monroe, North Carolina with vital resources, services, and opportunities. The project includes a resource directory, AI-powered resume builder, job application assistant, community events calendar, and volunteer opportunities.
